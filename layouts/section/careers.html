Job Offer - Maintenance / GPS Installation Technician

Ensenda, Baja California or Manzanillo, Colima


Helgen Technologies de México is looking for two highly skilled and committed Maintenance / GPS Installation Technicians to join our team. The selected candidates will play an essential role in maintaining and repairing operational technologies at port facilities in the cities of Ensenada, Baja California and Manzanillo, Colima, respectively.

Skills in electrical work, electronics, programming, and soldering are fundamental requirements for this position.

Responsibilities:

  • Install GPS on different types of units.
  • Perform preventive and corrective maintenance on equipment and operational technologies.
  • Carry out electronics work, wiring, and painting as needed on electronic and mechanical components.
  • Identify and resolve issues efficiently and effectively to minimize downtime.
  • Collaborate with the maintenance team to perform scheduled repair and maintenance tasks.
  • Conduct regular inspections to detect wear, defects, or potential issues in the equipment.
  • Contribute to maintaining accurate maintenance records and report the status of repairs and improvements.
  • Comply with safety standards and current regulations in all maintenance activities.
  • Work with other departments to ensure efficient operation of operational technologies.

Requirements:

  • Experience in GPS installation (wiring, electrical installation, platform monitoring).
  • Experience in electrical/electronic installations.
  • Previous experience in industrial maintenance or a similar field, with an emphasis on electronics.
  • Proven ability to perform electrical and electronic work accurately and safely.
  • Ability to diagnose and solve technical problems effectively.
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
  • Availability to work flexible hours, including night shifts and weekends if necessary.
  • Commitment to workplace safety and compliance with regulations.
  • Excellent oral and written communication skills.
  • Technical training in electricity/electronics is desirable.

We Offer:

  • Competitive salary based on the candidate’s experience and skills.
  • Legal benefits.
  • Opportunities for professional development and ongoing training.
  • Collaborative and innovation-driven work environment.

If you are a maintenance technician with strong electrical/electronic skills and want to be part of our team at Helgen Technologies de México, we invite you to send your resume and cover letter to: jobs@helgen.tech. Remember to mention in the email which position you are applying for: Ensenada, Baja California or Manzanillo, Colima.

We look forward to having you on our team to maintain and improve our operational technologies efficiently!
